Welcome from the Head

Welcome to TIME SCHOOL (Elementary)

I am proud to be the Head at TIME SCHOOL.
Thank you for visiting our website. I welcome this opportunity to introduce you to our school. At TIME’s, we strive to provide a secure, friendly and happy learning environment for all our children. The school currently has 200 pupils on roll and children are taught in single year group classes. The school is a caring community, supported by an energetic and committed staff. This, together with our hard working Governors, Parents' Association and parent volunteers ensures that the children are always the focus of school life and are valued both as an individual and as part of our whole school community. Our school is focused on promoting the children's academic, physical and spiritual development, thereby laying down good foundations for their continued progress. We follow a broad and balanced curriculum, where our aim is to enable each child to achieve their full potential. I hope that our website will provide a useful introduction to our school. If you have any questions regarding our school, or suggestions for the future development of our website, please contact me.

Soban Majeed
